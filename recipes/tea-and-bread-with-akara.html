Tea and Bread with Akara Recipe by Aceygram
30 mins

Akara, bread and tea is one of the most well liked of recent trending foods in the world. It is easy, it is quick, it tastes delicious. Itâ€™s appreciated by millions daily. Akara, bread and tea is something which a lot of people have loved their whole life. They are fine and they look wonderful

Akara and bread can be eaten in the morning for breakfast or evening as dinner, in Nigerian this super combo of food are street food usually seen by the roadside, except your taste bud is abnormal and your nostrils are completely blocked, the aroma coming from the hot Akara (bean cake) when those women are frying it is just too sweet. The sweet aroma coming from freshly baked bread will make you eat bread even when not planned for, the best way to enjoy bread and Akara is to stuff Akara in between two slices of bread or divide your Agege bread and place the Akara in the middle, Ahh! This is original Nigerian Akara burger

Ingredients

  • 3

    Cups of beans

  • 1

    Cup of vegetable oil

  • 3

    Seeds of fresh red pepper

  • 1/2

    Tbsp salt to taste

  • 1

    Medium onion

  • 1

    Agege bread

  • 3

    Tbsp ofMilo

  • 1

    Tin of milk

  • Water as needed

Steps

  • Wash the beans and wash off the back.rinse very well.

  • Cut onion,Fresh pepper inside and blend preferrable in the mill

  • Mix with little amount of water add salt to taste.

  • Heat up vegetable oil and use a small cooking spoon to scoop the mixture to the hot vegetable oil.

  • Fry till is golden brown repeat till the mixture finish.

  • Mix the milk and milo in a cup and pour in hot water stir.

  • Your delicious Tea and Bread with Akara is ready.
